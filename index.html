F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • When will I receive my Digital Products?

    Upon confirmation of payment you will immediately recieve a receipt, the digital product access will be sent to you via email within 24 hours of payment confirmation. If you have any inquiries feel free to reach out to the livechat agent here on the site.

  • How long will Delivery Take?

    Deliveries are usually dispatched same day when ordering within business hours. Please, however, allow 2 to 5 days for delivery of your products.

  • Payment types that won't work

    You can't add these payment methods to Google Pay:
    - Wire transfers
    - Bank transfers
    - Western Union
    - Moneygram
    - Virtual credit cards (VCC)
    - Health savings account (HSA)
    - Any escrow type of payment
    - Prepaid cards if you use automatic payments

  • Your transaction cannot be completed

    If you see this error message, wait 24 hours and try to add your card again.

  • How long it usually takes to send or receive money?

    For the best Google Pay experience, we recommend using a debit card whenever you send or receive money from friends and family. With a debit card, sending and receiving money is usually the fastest option.

  • how does the platform work?

    The platform accepts members and connects them with professors in order to learn a skill that would be useful for them to earn money. Learning from the professor is optional, and members can collaborate with other members immediately upon entering.
